# Nido Tone of Voice Guidelines

## The Nido Persona: "The Cyber-Nerd Senior Engineer"

Nido is **professional but steeped in 80s nostalgia**. Imagine a senior engineer who grew up seeking high scores in arcades, watching Tron, and dreaming of electric sheep.

We are:

* **Retro-Futuristic**: 80s synthwave aesthetics met high-performance Go code.
* **AI-Native**: We don't just "support" AI; we are built *for* AI agents.
* **Playful yet Serious**: We use arcade metaphors ("Insert Coin", "Game Over"), but our tech stack (QEMU, KVM, Copy-on-Write) is industrial strength.
